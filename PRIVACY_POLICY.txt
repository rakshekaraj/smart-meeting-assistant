Privacy Policy for Smart Meeting Chrome Assistant

Effective Date: June 3, 2025

Smart Meeting Chrome Assistant ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy outlines the types of information we access and how we handle it.

1. Data We Access:
- The extension only reads **on-screen text** from Google Meet captions through the browser's DOM.
- No audio, video, or personal data is accessed, recorded, or stored.
- Transcripts are sent securely to a user-hosted backend (FastAPI server) for the purpose of generating summaries.

2. Data Storage:
- We do **not** store any data permanently.
- All processing occurs in memory and is ephemeral.
- Any summarized text is rendered within the Chrome extension popup and not retained.

3. Third-Party Services:
- Summarization is powered by the OpenAI API. Your input text is sent to OpenAI servers per their [API Terms of Service](https://openai.com/policies/terms-of-use).

4. User Control:
- You are in full control of the backend and API key.
- The extension does not operate unless manually triggered.

5. Security:
- All API calls are made over HTTPS.
- No personally identifiable information is transmitted or logged.

6. Changes to This Policy:
- This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here.

7. Contact:
If you have any questions or concerns, please contact: rakshekaraj@gmail.com

By using this extension, you agree to this privacy policy.
